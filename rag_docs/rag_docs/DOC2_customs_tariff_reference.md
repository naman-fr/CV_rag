## SECTION G — COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What happens if I use the wrong HS Code?**
A: Customs will hold the shipment pending correction. A penalty of ₹25,000–₹2,00,000 may apply for intentional misdeclaration. SLA clock is paused during the hold.

**Q: Are there HS codes that automatically trigger physical inspection?**
A: Yes. Chemicals (Chapter 28–29), pharmaceuticals (Chapter 30), and certain food items (Chapters 7–12) may trigger random inspection. Allow 1–2 additional business days in your delivery estimate.

**Q: What is the duty calculation basis?**
A: Import duties are calculated on CIF value (Cost + Insurance + Freight). Export duties on FOB value.

**Q: What is the de minimis threshold for import?**
A: Shipments with CIF value below ₹5,000 are exempt from basic customs duty (not IGST).
